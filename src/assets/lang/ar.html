Qualification Process

Pass our training program and get certified

Step 1 - Qualification

The SKYTRADERS QUALIFICATION is the first step of the onboarding process. You need to succeed here to advance into the CONFIRMATION stage. Prove your trading skills and discipline in achieving your objectives.

Step 2 - Confirmation

The CONFIRMATION is the second and the last step to becoming a certified SKYTRADER. Once you pass the this stage and your results are verified by us, you will be offered to trade for our Proprietary Trading Firm.

Step 3 - Certified SKYTRADER

Congratulation! After successful completion of the previous two steps you will receive the SKYTRADERS CERTIFICATE confirming your trading skills.

With this certificate you are entitled to sign the Prop Trading agreement with us and start your career as a SKYTRADER at SKYTRADERS.
